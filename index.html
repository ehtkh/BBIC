FROM THE DESK OF MANAGER

It has been my long pending dream to work towards the development of my backward area specially in the sphere of education. When I put an end to all my industrial projects, I thought it is the perfect time to transform my dream into reality as I got enough time to spend. I founded Bright-Way Balika-inter college but soon I realized that there is still something a miss and my dream is yet to materialize. And this realization prompted me to establish another school that can keep pace with the educational development at the national level and hence founded Bangra Public School and when I did not find apt teachers in the surrounding hired them from other parts of our country. Now the school has been running successfully imparting best quality education of national standard in such a backward area.

THE DIFFERENT PARTS OF THE SCHOOL:

Apart from the conveying of essential knowledge and skills for living, the priceless ability and confidence to study, understand and apply any subject and environment in which children of all ages are encouraged to communicate freely and confidently at Bangra Public School. We strive to ensure that all students are happy throughout their time at the school and graduate feeling enthusiastic about life, having developed new friendships and gained sense of achievement in an ethical highly productive and motivated environment.
